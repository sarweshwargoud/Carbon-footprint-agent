Responsible AI Considerations

This Carbon Footprint Reduction Agent follows responsible AI principles:

1. Transparency
The system shows how emissions are calculated and which source contributes the most.

2. Fairness
The model does not judge or penalize users. It provides neutral sustainability advice.

3. Privacy
No personal data is stored. All calculations happen locally on the user’s device.

4. Safety
The system only provides environmental advice and does not give harmful or medical instructions.

5. Human Control
Users decide whether to follow the AI’s recommendations.
